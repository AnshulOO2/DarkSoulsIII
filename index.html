Dark Souls III

The Ashen One's Journey


The Ashen One embarks on a perilous quest to link the First Flame and restore balance to a world on the brink of darkness. Facing formidable foes and uncovering ancient secrets, the journey is fraught with danger and discovery.

Iudex Gundyr

The trial of fire for the Ashen One.

Iudex Gundyr is the first boss players face, a failed champion corrupted by darkness.

Vordt of the Boreal Valley

Frostbitten guardian of the High Wall.

Once a noble knight, Vordt was twisted into a monstrous beast by dark forces.

Curse-Rotted Greatwood

A corrupted tree writhing with undead growth.

A grotesque, cursed entity hidden deep within the Undead Settlement.

Crysltal Sage

A master of sorcery and illusion.

A powerful sorcerer who manipulates time and space with crystal magic.

Deacons Of The Deep

Fanatical priests serving the Deep.

A congregation of corrupted clerics who worship the abyssal Deep.

Abyss Watchers

Warriors eternally bound to fight the Abyss.

The Undead Legion of Farron, sworn to combat the corruption of the Abyss.

High Lord Wolnir

A fallen conqueror consumed by the Abyss.

A massive skeletal lord whose power comes from dark cursed artifacts.

Old Demon King

The last ruler of the ancient demons.

An ancient fire demon residing in the fiery depths of Smouldering Lake.

Pontiff Sulyvahn

The tyrant who reshaped Irithyll.

A ruthless sorcerer wielding twin blades and dark magic.

Yhorm the Giant

A tragic lord who once protected his people.

One of the Lords of Cinder, burdened by his own immense strength.

Aldrich, Devourer of Gods

The devourer who dreamed of the Deep.

A grotesque lord who consumes gods to sustain his power.

Dancer of the Boreal Valley

A silent executioner bound in rhythmic combat.

A fast and deadly knight whose movements resemble a deadly dance.

Oceiros, the Consumed King

A mad king obsessed with dragons.

A twisted former king who succumbed to madness and dragon worship.

Champion Gundyr

The true form of a legendary champion.

A perfected version of the fallen Iudex, far more aggressive and lethal.

Lothric & Lorian

The twin princes who refused the flame.

Two royal brothers bound together by curse and loyalty.

Ancient Wyvern

The guardian of Archdragon Peak.

A massive dragon defending the sacred land of ancient warriors.

The Nameless King

The forgotten god of war.

A legendary warrior who commands storms and dragons.

Soul of Cinder

The culmination of all who linked the Fire.

The final embodiment of all past Lords of Cinder.
